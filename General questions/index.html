General Questions

Do you accept all payment methods?

We accept a variety of payment methods, including credit cards, debit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay. We also offer financing options for eligible customers. Please check our payment options page for more information on the specific payment methods we accept and any associated fees or restrictions.

Do you support international shipping?

We currently only ship within the United States. We are working on expanding our shipping options to include international destinations in the near future. Please check back soon for updates on our shipping policies.

Do you use environmentally friendly packaging?

We are committed to using environmentally friendly packaging materials whenever possible. We use recycled and biodegradable materials for our packaging, and we strive to minimize our environmental impact in all aspects of our business. We also encourage our customers to recycle or reuse our packaging materials whenever possible. If you have any specific questions about our packaging materials or sustainability practices, please don't hesitate to contact us.
